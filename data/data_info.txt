Autism Dataset Information
==========================

Dataset: autism_dataset.csv
Total Samples: 1000
Features: 10 behavioral indicators
Target: Binary classification (0=No ASD, 1=ASD)

Features:
1. eye_contact - Does child make eye contact? (0/1)
2. responds_name - Responds to name? (0/1)
3. points_to_objects - Points to show interest? (0/1)
4. pretend_play - Engages in pretend play? (0/1)
5. repetitive_behaviour - Shows repetitive behaviors? (0/1)
6. sensory_sensitivity - Sensory sensitivity present? (0/1)
7. prefers_alone - Prefers solitary play? (0/1)
8. gestures - Uses gestures? (0/1)
9. delayed_speech - Speech delay? (0/1)
10. restricted_interests - Narrow/intense interests? (0/1)

Target Variable:
- label: 0 = No ASD, 1 = ASD indicators present

Class Distribution:
- Approximately balanced dataset
- ~50% positive cases, ~50% negative cases

Data Source: Synthetic behavioral assessment data
Usage: Training ML models for ASD screening
